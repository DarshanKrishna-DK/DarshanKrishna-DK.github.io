About us

We are WTM (Women Tech Makers), with 1.1k Ambassadors and around 81k members. We are representing WTM , aiming to encourage all women to take on vital roles in the field of technology.

Our mission transcends coding challenges, showcasing our fundamental commitment to championing and propelling women forward in the technical realm.

HerAura, is a women's Hackathon that extends beyond mere lines of code; it embodies a purposeful initiative to break down barriers, foster a strong support system, and enable women to thrive in the continuously changing tech domain .

Join us in this inspiring initiative where collaboration knows no bounds, creativity thrives, and mentorship shines.
